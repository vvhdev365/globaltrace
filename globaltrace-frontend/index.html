🛃 Need U.S. Customs Entry Status?

U.S. Customs data is NOT publicly available. No free API exists.

For entry status (format: XXX-XXXXXXX-X), you must:

1. Contact Your Customs Broker
The filer code (e.g., "8KP") identifies your broker. They have ACE access.
2. Use ACE Secure Data Portal
If you're a registered importer: ace.cbp.dhs.gov
3. Ask Your Freight Forwarder
They can check status through broker portals
